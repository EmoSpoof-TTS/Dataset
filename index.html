Can Emotion Fool Anti-spoofing?

(This work is submitted to Interspeech 2025)


------------------------------ Speech Samples ------------------------------


EmoSpoof-TTS is the dataset we introduce and release to facilitate research in impact of high-quality emotionally expressive synthetic speech on state-of-the-art anti-spoofing models. It contains a total of 36,000 synthesized speech samples from four emotions (Happiness, Anger, Sadness, and Neutral state), 10 (5 male, 5 female) speakers, and 3 TTS models (StyleTTS2, CosyVoice, F5-TTS). The bona-fide samples are from Emotional Speech Dataset (ESD).
